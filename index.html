🐠 Aquarium Monitor

Track your aquarium water parameters with historical charts and quality indicators

Available for iOS & Android — coming soon

What it does

🪣

Multiple Tanks

Manage freshwater and shrimp tank profiles with dimensions and volume.

🧪

Parameter Logging

Log pH, KH, GH, TDS, and Temperature after testing with your kit.

📈

Historical Charts

View trends over time with safe range bands highlighted on the chart.

🟢

Status Indicators

Color-coded status: green (good), orange (warning), red (danger).

📴

Fully Offline

All data stored locally on your device. No account needed.

🌙

Dark Mode

Follows your device's light/dark mode setting automatically.
