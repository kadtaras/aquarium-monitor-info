# Aquarium Monitor

A mobile app for tracking your aquarium water parameters with historical charts and quality indicators.

> Available for iOS and Android — coming soon

---

## What it does

- Manage multiple aquarium profiles (freshwater & shrimp tanks)
- Log water parameters after testing with your kit: pH, KH, GH, TDS, Temperature
- View historical charts with safe range indicators
- Color-coded status: green (good), orange (warning), red (danger)
- Works fully offline — no account needed
- Dark mode support
